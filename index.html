About Me

My journey began with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science Engineering, where my passion for programming flourished. I delved into languages such as Java, Kotlin, and JavaScript, nurturing a keen interest in mobile development. In 2018, I embarked on the exciting path of Android app development, translating programming concepts into user-friendly applications.

Taking a significant leap in 2022, I relocated to Canada to pursue further education. I enrolled in a Post-Graduation program in Mobile Application Design and Development at Lambton College. This experience was transformative, providing me with advanced knowledge in both Android and iOS app development.

Armed with a comprehensive skill set and a solid educational foundation, I am passionate about utilizing modern architectures, unified codebases, and platform optimizations to bring ideas to life.
