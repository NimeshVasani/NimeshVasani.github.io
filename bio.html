About Me

The Full Story

"Turning vision into reality through focus, determination, and code."

I feel incredibly fortunate to have forged a fulfilling career doing what I love. From a young age, I harbored a clear vision of my professional future, and driven by focus and determination, I set out to turn that vision into reality.

My journey began with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science Engineering, where my passion for programming flourished. I delved into languages such as Java, Kotlin, and JavaScript, nurturing a keen interest in mobile development. In 2018, I embarked on the exciting path of Android app development, translating my programming skills into tangible, user-friendly applications.

Taking a significant leap in 2022, I relocated to Canada to pursue further education. I enrolled in a Post-Graduation program in Mobile Application Design and Development at Lambton College. This experience was transformative, providing me with advanced knowledge in both Android and iOS app development.

Throughout this academic journey, I honed my skills, gaining expertise that extends beyond the basics. The challenges I encountered and overcame during my time in Canada have significantly enriched my perspective and added layers to my professional capabilities.

Now, armed with a comprehensive skill set and a solid educational foundation, I am eager to contribute my expertise to meaningful projects. Having worked on inspiring endeavors and collaborated with influential and creative individuals, I firmly believe in the synergy created when talented minds come together.

If you are interested in learning more about my experience or exploring potential collaborations on upcoming projects, please don't hesitate to get in touch. I look forward to the opportunity to discuss how my unique background and skills can contribute to the success of future endeavors.
